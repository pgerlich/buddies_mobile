About

First and foremost, my name is Tina Gerlich and I am a Dynamics GP specialist. I also have extensive experience with various aspects of SQL.

I started Gerlich Consulting in March of 2014 to keep up with the ins and outs of Dynamics GP. Since then, I've taken on several contracts to fulfill that goal. As I've worked over the last 14 years in the field, I have gained a strong technical understanding of Dynamics and DB management - from data validation to mass migrations - and everything in between.

Now I'm looking to expand my horizons, continuing work in Dynamics GP - and extending out into SQL development. I am looking to take on more contracts as I pursue that goal. Check out the work that I've done, and don't hesitate to reach out if you think I would be a good fit for an upcoming project!
